Privacy Policy

Who we are

What we collect

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google. If you use the app without signing in, we collect nothing — progress is stored locally on your device only.

Why we collect it

Solely to sync your study progress across devices. Your name is displayed in the app; your email identifies your account.

What we do not collect

  • Passwords — Google handles all authentication
  • Financial data, location, contacts, or browsing history
  • Any data from users who do not sign in

Where data is stored

Signed-in progress is stored on Supabase (supabase.co), which runs on AWS infrastructure. Access is restricted by database-level row-level security — only your own account can read or write your data.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party.

Age requirement

You must be 13 or older to create an account. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe an under-13 account exists, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Deleting your data

To delete your account and all associated data, email jaeheejin10@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my data". We will process it within 30 days.
